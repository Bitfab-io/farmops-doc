# Farm operators

## Farm operator board in Trello

[img]

* **Everyday tasks**. Main farm operator tasks.
* **Printing queue**. Used to assign or write down printing jobs to be done by the operator.
* **Small extra tasks**. Used to assign or write down other small tasks to be done by the operator.
* **Small projects**. Other projects or task sets assigned to the operator that may span for several days or weeks. Use the checklist to create the individual tasks.
* **Big project #1, #2, etc** Project assigned to the operator that may span for several months.
