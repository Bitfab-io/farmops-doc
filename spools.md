# Spools

## Filament tangling

Only 3 positions valid positions for the filament end

* In the spool end
* In a printer extruder / ptfe liner
* In your hand

Always cut the end that the manufacturer leaves in the spool holes. This can cause a tangle.

Myth: manufacturers supply tangled spools.
